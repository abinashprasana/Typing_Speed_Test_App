
It is better to be feared than loved, if you cannot be both.

Men must be either caressed or annihilated.

The lion cannot protect himself from traps, and the fox cannot defend himself from wolves.
One must therefore be a fox to recognize traps, and a lion to frighten wolves.

A prince must learn how not to be good.

Politics have no relation to morals.

Everyone sees what you appear to be, few experience what you really are.

The ends justify the means.

He who builds on the people builds on mud.

A wise ruler ought never to keep faith when by doing so it would be against his interests.

Fortune is a woman, and it is necessary to beat and coerce her to submit.

The promise given was a necessity of the past: the word broken is a necessity of the present.

Never attempt to win by force what can be won by deception.

You ought never to suffer disorder to continue so as to avoid war, for war is not avoided thereby but is only deferred to your disadvantage.

The first method for estimating the intelligence of a ruler is to look at the men he has around him.
